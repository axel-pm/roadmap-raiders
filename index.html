How to Play

Goal

Reach Level 10 by defeating PM monsters! Each monster defeated levels you up.

Your Deck

You start with 5 Hero cards (podcast guests) in your hand. Each has Attack, Defense, and Wisdom stats.

Combat

Each turn, a monster appears. Play hero cards from your hand to fight it. Your total Attack must beat the monster's combined stats to win.

Treasures

Defeating monsters earns treasure cards that boost your stats or give special abilities.

Special Abilities

Each hero has a unique ability based on their podcast topics. Use them wisely!

Losing

If you run out of HP (start with 20) or can't defeat a monster, the game ends.
